Chris Neels

Chris is an experienced product strategist, manager, and researcher.

Previously, at the consultancies Idea Couture and Deloitte, Chris advised companies including FedEx, Ford Motor Company, Suncor, Johnson & Johnson, J.M. Smucker, and Tyson on strategic innovation, human-centered design, and agile development.

Over the past year, Chris has been researching the social construction and implications of artificial intelligence at University College London. He is currently writing a book that applies responsible innovation (RI) and science and technology studies (STS) to help organizations design and develop emerging technologies with society, systems, and the long-term in mind.

Chris holds an MSc in Science and Technology Studies from University College London, and a BCom in Business Technology Management from the UBC Sauder School of Business.
