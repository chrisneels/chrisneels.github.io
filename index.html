Hello, I'm Chris and I am a product strategist, manager, and researcher.

I recently launched 2100.design, a service helping organizations anticipate and shape the wider effects of their technology.

In the past, I worked as a tech and innovation consultant, advising companies across a range of industries on strategic innovation, human-centered design, and agile development.

Leading up to 2100.design, I did postgraduate research at University College London studying the social construction and effects of artificial intelligence. My studies were interdisciplinary – drawing from STS, anthropology, sociology, philosophy, and history. My dissertation was on the topic of applied responsible innovation.
